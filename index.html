Smart Light Control

Compatible with Philips Hue, LIFX, and Nanoleaf. Mix multiple brands and bridges for a broad range of visual experiences.

Precision Music Sync

Connect to Spotify, Apple Music, or the microphone. The algorithm knows in advance how to sync lights to the beat for a perfectly timed show.

Save your favorite configurations and launch them instantly with iOS Shortcuts. Group your smart lights for synchronized displays.

Automatically calibrate your lights to your music using the microphone input on iOS.
