Beautiful Effects

iLightShow automatically creates a light show that is synchronized with your favorite songs. You can customize the light show with a variety of options to create the perfect experience for any occasion.

  • Beats and Drums - lights react to beats and drums for dynamic visual effects

  • Buildups and Drops - dramatic strobes during exciting parts of the song

  • Automatic calibration - using the microphone input for a perfect sync

  • Color effects - strobes, flashes, pulses, slow transitions...

  • Mood detection - adjusts the effects' intensity to match the music's mood

  • Customizable - tailor your light show with adjustable brightness, color palettes, flashes, strobes, pulses, and effect sizes
